# DSA-3050_F
Problem Statement

Many students in online learning environments fail or withdraw from courses. This project aims to analyze how student demographics, academic background, and online engagement influence final course outcomes (Pass, Fail, Withdrawn, Distinction) using the OULAD dataset.

Objectives
	1.	To examine the relationship between demographic factors (gender, age, education level, region, disability) and final results.
	2.	To analyze how academic factors (previous attempts, credits studied, assessment scores) affect performance.
	3.	To evaluate whether VLE engagement (clicks and activity) influences student success.
	4.	To identify patterns of at-risk students who are likely to fail or withdraw.
